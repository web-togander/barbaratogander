An Unsolved Blank Paradise

Improvisation duo with Paula Shocron in tribute to the great pianist and improviser, Connie Crothers, including records of her voice and piano, as well as the poem "Anonymous Landscape for Connie Crothers" by Yuko Otomo.

Paula Shocron: piano, objects and voice
Barbara Togander: voice and turntable
