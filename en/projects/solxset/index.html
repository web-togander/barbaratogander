SOLXSET

Fragments, collage of sounds and intentions, familiar and unrecognizable, referential and brand new, as when changing language, climate, landscape, environment, habits, food, smells, houses, roads, jungles, deserts and forests, languages again, architectures.
Everything at the same time in a constant movement, without a moment for reflection, without understanding.
And chance as the thread that weaves all these universes and rearranges them in another chaotic inevitability.

Barbara Togander: voice, vinyls and electronics
