Perccontrvozcorn¿.?

This project was conceived and realized as a show at the Batato Barea Theater of the Ricardo Rojas Cultural Center under the curatorship of Rubén Szuchmacher. A sound staging based on the idea of spatial and musical de-construction, where convention mutate into other possibilities: the musicians scattered among the seats, the audience on the stage, changes of axis, a video that directs the music, barbie dolls starring silences.

The album is the sonic result of this project.
